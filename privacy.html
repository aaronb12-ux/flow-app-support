Flow App Privacy Policy

Effective Date: September 2025

Data Collection

Flow collects and stores the following information:

  • Tasks you create and their completion status
  • Expense entries and amounts
  • Account information for app functionality

Data Storage

Your data is securely stored using Supabase, a third-party database service. Supabase maintains industry-standard security practices to protect your information.

Data Usage

We use your data solely to:

  • Provide app functionality (task management and expense tracking)
  • Sync your data across devices
  • Display your 30-day history

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes. Your data may be processed by Supabase as our database provider.

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your data. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

Account Deletion

You can delete your account and all associated data at any time through the app's settings menu.

Third-Party Services

Flow uses Supabase for data storage. Please review Supabase's privacy policy at https://supabase.com/privacy

Contact

Questions about this privacy policy? Contact us at flowapp2025@gmail.com

This privacy policy may be updated periodically. Continued use constitutes acceptance of changes.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a task?

Tap the "+" button on the main screen and enter your task.

How do I track an expense?

Navigate to the Finance tab and tap "Add Expense".

How do I view my 30-day history?

Tap the "History" button to see your completed tasks and expenses from the past month.

How do I delete a task or expense?

Swipe left on any item and tap the delete button.

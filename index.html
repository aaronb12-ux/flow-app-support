Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a task?

Tap the "+" button on the top right on the main screen and enter your task.

How do I track an expense?

Tap the wallet icon on the top right of the purchases tab.

How do I view my 30-day history?

Tap the "History" button to see your completed tasks and expenses from the past month. Press and hold on a day to see your tasks and purchases made that day.

How do I delete a task or expense?

Hold down on the task/purchase
